About Us

Since 2000

The aroma of hot plates of mouthwatering Mexican cuisine welcomes you upon entering this Mexican Restaurant. With so many fresh authentic Mexican dishes to choose from, the hardest part of your visit will be picking just one! Obviously, you will have to try the cheese dip with 7 Tequilas fresh made tortillas and salsa. You’ll savor not only the food but also the terrific ambiance. The weekday specials at 7 Tequilas make it a ‘must stop’ on your dining adventures throughout the week.
